# CAT Prep Tracker

**Your personal command center for CAT exam preparation.**

CAT Prep Tracker is a free, lightweight web app built for serious CAT aspirants who want to stay on top of their preparation without the noise. No sign-ups, no subscriptions — just open it and start tracking.

---

## What it does

Stay organized across every dimension of your CAT prep:

- **Section & Topic Tracking** — Break your syllabus into sections (QA, DILR, VARC) and individual topics. Check off topics as you master them and watch your progress bars fill up in real time.

- **Study Log** — Record every study session with the date, section, hours spent, and notes on what you covered. Build a detailed history of your preparation over time.

- **Mock Test Log** — Log every mock test with your score and percentile. Track your improvement and spot patterns in your performance.

- **Streak Tracker** — Stay consistent with a daily study streak counter. See your current streak, longest streak, and total days studied — just enough motivation to keep going.

- **Activity Heatmap** — A 90-day visual heatmap shows your study activity at a glance, so you can see exactly where you've been putting in the work (and where you haven't).

- **Charts & Analytics** — Visualize your mock score trend over time, study hours broken down by section, section-wise completion, and your daily hours over the last 30 days.

- **Topic Notes** — Attach personal notes to any topic — formulas, shortcuts, weak areas, reminders. Everything you need, right where you need it.

- **Exam Countdown** — Set your exam date and see exactly how many days you have left, every time you open the app.

---

## Who it's for

Any CAT aspirant who wants a simple, distraction-free way to track their preparation — whether you're just starting out or in the final stretch before the exam.

---

## Built with simplicity in mind

No internet required after the first load. No account needed. Your data stays on your device. Just a single HTML file that works in any browser.
